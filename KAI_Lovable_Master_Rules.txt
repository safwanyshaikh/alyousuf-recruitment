ACTIVE JOBS vs CAMPAIGNS

ACTIVE JOBS

* Small requirements
* Day-to-day hiring
* No fixed interview schedule
* Continuous sourcing
* Continuous submissions

CAMPAIGNS

* Mass hiring
* Interview drives
* Client visit events
* Online interview campaigns
* Fixed dates
* Fixed locations
* Large quantities

JD Parser should eventually ask:

"Create Active Job or Campaign?"

when quantity crosses configured threshold.
